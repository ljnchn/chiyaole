# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core visual characteristics of our design system, derived directly from our design tokens.

## Brand Colors
Our palette is carefully chosen to reflect our brand identity.

-   **Primary Color:** `#007AFF` - A vibrant blue, used for primary calls to action, important links, and key interactive elements.
-   **Secondary Color:** `#34C759` - A fresh green, providing support for less prominent actions, status indicators, or secondary information.
-   **Tertiary Color:** `#5856D6` - A rich purple, serving as an accent for highlights, badges, or decorative touches.
-   **Neutral Color:** `#F2F2F7` - A soft off-white, forming the foundation for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic UI elements.

## Typography
We utilize a distinct typographic scale to ensure readability and hierarchy.

-   **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for all major headings, providing a modern and impactful presence.
-   **Body Text:** `inter` - Applied to all paragraph text and general content, ensuring clarity and legibility at various sizes.
-   **Labels:** `inter` - Employed for form labels, buttons, and other interactive elements, maintaining consistency with body text for functional elements.

## Shape and Form
The geometry of our UI elements contributes to the overall feel of the product.

-   **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate) - Corners of UI elements have a moderate roundedness, striking a balance between modern softness and structured design.

## Spacing
Our spacing system creates visual breathing room and defines relationships between elements.

-   **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - A standard level of whitespace is applied within and between UI elements, ensuring a balanced and comfortable visual density.
